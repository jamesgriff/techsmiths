Break into tech

The skills and experience you have gained in previous roles allow you to bring something extra to the table and give you more dimension as a developer than someone who went though a more "traditional" route into coding.

Be supported

We know it's scary to think about changing career but we'll be there to support you throughout the whole process. From learning the skills necessary to be confident at coding, to finding a job placement after completing the course; we will be there to support you every step of the way.

FAQ

What does the course cost?

Nothing! The 12 week bootcamp is free followed by an 18 month contract at competitive London salary rates.

How long is the course?

The course is 12 weeks of training, followed by a year's job placement in industry with the option of making that a permanent role.

Do you need any previous coding experience?

No! We will provide all of the training for you to become confident in software development with no previous experience required.

Where are you based?

The first 12 weeks will be held in our offices in north London.
